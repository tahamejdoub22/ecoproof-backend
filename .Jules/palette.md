# Palette's Journal - UX & Accessibility Learnings

## 2024-05-22 - [Developer Experience is UX too]
**Learning:** In backend-only projects, Swagger UI is the primary "User Interface" for developers. Enhancing it improves the "user" experience significantly.
**Action:** Always enable search (`filter: true`) and collapse tags (`docExpansion: 'none'`) in Swagger for cleaner documentation.
